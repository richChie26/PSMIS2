Welcome to Property and Supply Management Information System

A system to manage all government property and supplies in one centralized system. It provides complete information and enables efficient tracking, monitoring, and reporting of inventory levels, transactions, and usage. This system offers increased accuracy, improved asset utilization, and streamlined workflows, resulting in better cost control and higher operational efficiency.
